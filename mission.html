Our Mission Statement

At St. Francis Day and Boarding Primary School, our mission is to "Educate to Build the Nation" by providing quality education that nurtures holistic development. We are committed to:

  • Fostering academic excellence through innovative teaching methods
  • Developing well-rounded individuals with strong moral values
  • Creating a supportive environment that encourages creativity and critical thinking
  • Building future leaders who will contribute positively to society
  • Promoting cultural awareness and global citizenship
  • Ensuring equal opportunities for all students to reach their full potential
