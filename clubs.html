Our School Clubs

At St. Francis Day and Boarding Primary School, we believe in holistic education that goes beyond academic excellence. Our diverse range of clubs provides students with opportunities to explore their interests, develop new skills, and build lasting friendships.

1. Soccer Club

Available for both Primary and Pre-Primary Sections

Join our soccer club to develop teamwork, leadership, and physical fitness while having fun on the field.

2. Scouts Club

Available for Primary Section only

Learn essential life skills, outdoor activities, and leadership through our scouting program.

3. Red Cross Club

Available for Primary Section only

Develop first aid skills and learn about health and humanitarian values.

4. Wildlife Club

Available for Primary Section only

Explore nature, learn about conservation, and develop environmental awareness.

5. Public Speech Club

Available for Primary Section only

Enhance communication skills, build confidence, and develop public speaking abilities.

6. Cookery Club

Available for Primary Section only

Learn cooking skills, nutrition basics, and explore different cuisines.
