About St. Francis Day and Boarding Primary School

Established in 1989, St. Francis Day and Boarding Primary School has been a cornerstone of education in Ntinda. Our mission is to "Educate to Build the Nation" by providing quality education that nurtures holistic development. Over the years, we have contributed significantly to the community by producing well-rounded individuals who excel in various fields.

The School is well located in urban Nakawa division along the Ntinda-Kiwatule/Naalya-road where it has sprouted to being one of the best primary schools in terms of academics, infrastructure and a good administrative structure that has been appreciated by many.
